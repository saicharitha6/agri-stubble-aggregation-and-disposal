ABOUT US

Agriculture, which is one of the primary activity of production. It acts as a supporting service for both secondary and tertiary activities. On harvesting, along side the specified crop so produced, tons of residue is additionally generated which can be termed as stubble which is crop residue. Usually farmers do not have enough time to make the fields ready for next crop and hence they prefer the cheap and easy way of disposing stubble, that is burning the stubble which has lot of negative effect in environment and eco-system. The main objective of our application provides an effective means of collecting the stubble from the farmers and produce biogas from the stubble. The produced biogas and crop residue is sell and buy by our end-users.
